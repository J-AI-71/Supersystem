Terms

These are simple, plain-language terms for the free SafeShare web app.

1) What SafeShare does

  • SafeShare removes common tracking parameters from URLs locally in your browser.
  • SafeShare does not upload your links to a server for processing.

2) No warranty

  • The app is provided “as is”.
  • We do not guarantee that a cleaned link will work in every case.

3) Your responsibility

  • Some parameters are functional (language, product IDs, filters) or used for attribution (affiliate links).
  • Use SafeShare responsibly and verify critical links.

4) Privacy

See the Privacy page for details.

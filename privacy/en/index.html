Privacy Policy

SafeShare is built for link hygiene: removing common tracking parameters from URLs locally in your browser. This page explains what data is processed when you use SafeShare and why.

Local-first: cleaning happens on your device No accounts, no sign-in No analytics embedded by SafeShare No link uploads
Last updated: 2026-01-18

1) Controller / Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact: listings@safesharepro.com

Note: This is a product privacy statement for SafeShare’s website/app pages. It is not legal advice.

2) What SafeShare processes in your browser (local-first)

When you paste a URL into SafeShare, the URL is processed in your browser to remove certain query parameters (e.g. utm_*, fbclid, gclid). SafeShare does not send your pasted links to SafeShare servers as part of the cleaning process.

  • Input URL (pasted by you): processed locally to compute a cleaned URL.
  • Cleaned URL: shown to you; you can copy or share it.
If you use the Share button, your device/browser may open its native share dialog. SafeShare does not control what other apps do with a link you share.

3) Local storage (optional “Impact” stats)

If you use the “Impact” feature (stats such as “links cleaned” or “parameters removed”), SafeShare stores only aggregated counters in your browser’s local storage (localStorage).

  • Stored: counters/totals (e.g. number of cleaned links)
  • Not stored: your URLs, domains, link history

You can reset these stats on the App page (Reset stats).

4) Cookies

SafeShare does not set tracking cookies for analytics or advertising. Your browser or hosting infrastructure may use technically necessary cookies (e.g. for security features).

5) Server logs / hosting (website access)

Like most websites, the hosting and delivery infrastructure may process technical access data when you visit pages (e.g. IP address, date/time, requested page, user agent, referrer, response status). This is typically required to deliver the website, prevent abuse, and maintain security.

SafeShare aims to keep server-side data minimal. The link-cleaning itself happens locally and does not require uploading links.

6) Third-party services

SafeShare does not embed third-party analytics trackers (e.g. Google Analytics) by default. If you follow external links (e.g. Payhip purchase pages), those third parties process data under their own policies.

  • Payhip (purchase links): external site; their privacy policy applies.

7) Legal basis (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, processing for delivering the website and preventing abuse is generally based on legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) and/or performance of a contract / pre-contractual steps (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR), depending on the situation.

8) Your rights

If GDPR applies, you may have rights such as access, rectification, deletion, restriction, objection, and data portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Contact: listings@safesharepro.com

9) Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the product or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates the current version.

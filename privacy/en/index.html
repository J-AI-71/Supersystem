Privacy

SafeShare is built for data minimization: it cleans links locally in your browser. There are no accounts and no link uploads.

Short version: local-first → the link stays on your device. SafeShare does not operate a “URL cleaner” server.
What we collect Local storage Third parties Limits Contact

What SafeShare collects

SafeShare App (web)

  • No account required
  • No link upload (cleaning happens in your browser)
  • No server-side tracking by SafeShare inside the app code
  • SafeShare does not build user profiles
Your link is processed by JavaScript on your device. The cleaned result is shown to you and can be copied/shared by you.

Website infrastructure

Like most websites, hosting/CDN providers may process basic technical requests to deliver the page (e.g. IP address, timestamps, user agent) as part of normal web delivery.

Important distinction: SafeShare itself does not run a “URL cleaning backend” that receives your links. The cleaning is local. Any infrastructure logs are about page delivery, not about the URLs you paste into the app.

Local storage (on-device)

Some pages can store small settings in your browser (for example an optional Impact counter). This uses your device storage (e.g. localStorage).

What is stored

  • Only totals (e.g. “links cleaned”, “parameters removed”)
  • No URLs
  • No domains
  • No cross-device sync

How to clear it

  • Use the in-app “Reset stats” button (if visible)
  • Or clear website data in your browser settings
If you disable or clear storage, SafeShare still works — only convenience features reset.

Third parties and external links

SafeShare may link to third-party sites (for example documentation, directory listings, or payment pages). When you open those, their own privacy policies apply.

When you open a cleaned link

The destination website may still track you via cookies, fingerprinting, login state, or server logs. SafeShare cannot control that.

Payments / purchases

If you purchase SafeShare products via an external payment provider, the provider processes payment data. SafeShare does not receive your card details.

What SafeShare does not promise

SafeShare is not an “invisibility” tool. It is a concrete hygiene step: removing common tracking parameters before sharing.

What it prevents

  • Sharing campaign parameters (e.g. utm_*)
  • Sharing click IDs (e.g. gclid, fbclid)
  • Passing along extra tracking “baggage” in URLs

What it cannot prevent

  • Tracking scripts on the destination website
  • Server logs at the destination
  • Account-based tracking if you are logged in
  • Fingerprinting techniques
Tip: If a cleaned link breaks, a parameter may have been functional (e.g. language, filters, product IDs, affiliate attribution). SafeShare is conservative by default; advanced control (allowlists/policies/audit) belongs in Pro.

Privacy

SafeShare is built for data minimization: link cleaning happens locally in your browser. You paste a link, SafeShare removes common tracking parameters, and you copy/share the cleaned link.

Open the App Help Start
No panic, no “invisible mode” promises. This is a concrete hygiene step: share less tracking ballast.

What SafeShare does

  • Removes common tracking parameters (e.g. utm_*, gclid, fbclid).
  • Runs local-first: processing happens in your browser.
  • Does not require creating an account.

What SafeShare does NOT do

  • It does not stop server logs on the destination site (IP, timestamp, user-agent).
  • It does not block cookies/fingerprinting scripts on the destination site.
  • It does not make you “invisible”.

Desktop-Konsole (optional)

Alternativ in der Browserkonsole (Desktop) ausführen:

localStorage.setItem('ss_publisher','dealblog');
localStorage.setItem('ss_wl','partnerA');

Zurücksetzen:

localStorage.removeItem('ss_publisher');
localStorage.removeItem('ss_wl');

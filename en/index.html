SafeShare

Clean links & unwrap redirects — locally in your browser. No accounts, no server logs.

Open App · See Pro · Quickstart

What SafeShare does

  • Clean: removes tracking parameters like utm_, gclid, fbclid.
  • Unwrap: replaces redirect chains with a clear destination (e.g., google.com/url, t.co …).
  • Whitelist: keep legitimate parameters (e.g., tag, ref) via Team-Whitelist.
  • Privacy-by-Design: 100% local in your browser, no external requests.

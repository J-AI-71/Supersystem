Tracking parameters out. Share clean links.

Many links include extra labels (utm_*, fbclid, gclid …). Useful for analytics, but often just ballast when sharing. SafeShare removes common tracking keys locally in your browser.

Local-first: cleaning happens in your browser. No upload.
Important: this is not an “invisibility” promise. It’s a practical hygiene step for sharing.

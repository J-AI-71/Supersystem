Share clean links

Many links contain extra tracking data — like labels stuck to a package. Useful for analytics, unnecessary when you forward or share.

Open App Help Pro
SafeShare removes common tracking parameters locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. It doesn’t make you “invisible” — it simply reduces link ballast.

What SafeShare does

Removes tracking parameters

Examples: utm_*, gclid, fbclid, msclkid, _ga …

Local-first by design

Cleaning happens in your browser. No servers. No logs. No accounts required.

Share workflow

Copy a link → clean it → paste/share the clean result (works well with messengers and email).

Pro for control

Audit what was removed, allowlists/policies, and more careful rules for edge cases.

Don’t blindly remove “everything”: affiliate/ref parameters (ref, tag, …) can be intentional. Use Pro if you need fine control.

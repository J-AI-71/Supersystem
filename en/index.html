Many links contain extra “stickers” (utm_*, fbclid, gclid …). Useful for analytics — but often unnecessary baggage when you share.

Why it’s worth it (no drama)

Respect

You share less extra data that others didn’t choose.

Cleaner communication

Links look more trustworthy and less “marketing-ish”.

Fewer breakages

Some apps shorten or wrap long URLs in unhelpful ways.

Local-first

No need to paste your link into an online cleaner service.

Don’t remove everything blindly

Affiliate / partner parameters

Some parameters are attribution (e.g. ref, tag). Removing them can break revenue attribution.

Pro: per-domain allowlists and policies.

Functional parameters

Some keys control content (language, filters, product IDs, search). Good tools are conservative and don’t delete everything.

App: common tracking only. Pro: audit + rules.

Remove tracking parameters.
Share clean links.

Many links contain extra information — like stickers (utm_*, fbclid, gclid …). Useful for analytics, but often unnecessary when forwarding.

Open the app Deutsch
SafeShare is local-first: the cleaning happens in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. No accounts.
Important: this is not an “invisibility” promise. It’s a practical hygiene step before sharing.

In 10 seconds

  1. Copy a link
  2. Paste it into SafeShare
  3. Clean → copy → share

Before

https://example.com/article?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch&fbclid=XYZ

After

https://example.com/article

What SafeShare removes (web app)

  • utm_* (campaign parameters)
  • Click IDs like gclid, fbclid, msclkid, igshid
  • other common tracking keys (e.g. _ga, mc_cid, mc_eid, srsltid)
Don’t remove everything blindly: affiliate/partner keys (e.g. ref, tag) can control attribution/revenue.

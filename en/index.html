Remove tracking parameters.
Share clean links.

Many URLs contain extra “labels” like utm_*, fbclid, gclid and more. Useful for analytics, but often unnecessary when forwarding links.

Open the app Learn: tracking parameters Pro (advanced)
SafeShare is local-first: cleaning happens in your browser. Links are not uploaded.
Important: this is not an “invisibility” promise. It’s a practical link-hygiene step when sharing.

In 10 seconds

  1. Copy a link
  2. Paste it into the SafeShare app
  3. Clean → copy → share

Why this helps (no drama)

Respect

You share fewer extras that other people didn’t choose.

Looks cleaner

Shorter links feel less “ad-tech” and more trustworthy.

Less breakage

Some apps mangle long URLs. Clean links are often more robust.

Local-first

No need to paste into random online cleaners.

Don’t remove everything blindly

Affiliate / partner keys

Some parameters are attribution (e.g. ref, tag). Removing them can break attribution/revenue.

Pro: allowlists/policies per domain.

Functional parameters

Some keys control content (language, filters, product IDs, search). Good tools stay conservative.

App: common tracking only. Pro: audit + rules.

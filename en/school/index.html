Clean links without panic

Tracking parameters are extra data attached to a link — like labels. Often unnecessary when you share a link with other people.

Clean a link now Pro (audit + allowlists)
SafeShare cleans typical tracking keys locally in your browser. No upload.

What those “labels” look like

https://example.com/article?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch
https://shop.example.com/product?gclid=ABC123&fbclid=XYZ987

When not to remove blindly

  • Affiliate/ref: parameters like ref, tag can be intentional.
  • Functional keys: language, filters, product IDs can be required.
SafeShare is link hygiene. It does not make you “invisible”.
